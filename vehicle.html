Two Wheeler Relocation

Moveroo provides end-to-end two wheeler relocation for bikes and scooters, ensuring safety, convenience, and peace of mind. Our team handles everything from pickup at your doorstep to secure delivery at your destination, with options tailored to your needs and budget.

By Train

For cost-effective and reliable long-distance moves, we offer IRCTC-approved rail transport for two wheelers. Your bike or scooter is carefully packed, documented, and loaded into the railway parcel or luggage compartment. Our team manages all paperwork and ensures safe loading/unloading at both ends. This mode is ideal for budget-conscious customers and those moving across states.

By Road

Your two wheeler is picked up from your home, expertly packed (if required), and loaded onto a specialized bike carrier or mini-truck. Our trained handlers ensure your vehicle is secured and protected from damage. The vehicle is then transported directly to your new address, with real-time tracking and updates provided throughout the journey. This is the fastest and most flexible option for city-to-city or interstate moves.

Four Wheeler Relocation

Moveroo's four wheeler relocation service is designed for total convenience and maximum safety, whether you're moving a family car, SUV, or luxury vehicle. We offer two premium options to suit your preferences:

Chauffeur Driven

A professional, background-verified driver collects your car from your location and drives it to your new city or address. This service is ideal for those who want a personal touch, minimal handling, and the flexibility to move at your schedule. Your car is driven with utmost care, and you receive live updates throughout the journey. All drivers follow strict safety protocols and your vehicle is fully insured during transit.

Loading on Carrier

For long-distance or multi-car moves, your vehicle is loaded onto a specialized car carrier (open or closed, as per your choice). Our team handles all loading, securing, and documentation. The carrier is GPS-tracked and monitored until safe delivery at your destination. This is the safest option for luxury, new, or multiple vehicles, and is fully insured against transit risks.
